Welcome

About

I (Jeannie Rodriguez) am the President and sole employee of the Copy Cat Cook. I have been working with small organizations by providing quality service since 2015.

I live in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania with my husband, two yound men, and a bichon (Buddy). We lived here all our lives. Philadelphia is a great city and offers alot of small cafe's and venues for a foodie like me. We love to take advantage of all the great things a city like Philadelphia has to offer and have been extremely happy with our choice and the community in which we live.
