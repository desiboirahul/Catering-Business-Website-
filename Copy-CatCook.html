Welcome

Here's my website I created along with my class mates for Copy Cat Cook in Philadelphia, Pa. This website needed a unique look to help promote the fantastic food that I as a chef prepares. Copy Cat Cook specializes in catering and event planning in Philadelphia, Pa. Not only can Copy Cat Cook manage their menus on the website, they can also manage their cooking classes.
